About Me

I'm actively looking for jobs in 2018!

I am currently a 5th year PhD candidate at the Transformation, Interpretation, and Analysis of Language (TIAL) lab at University of Washington, Electrical Engineering Department. I am working with Prof. Mari Ostendorf on speech and natural language processing.

Before joining University of Washington, I worked on compressed sensing with Prof. Sergiy A. Vorobyov and Prof. Hai Jiang at Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, University of Alberta, Canada. I obtained my Bachelor degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China.
